Help & FAQs

How do I book a tour package?

You can browse our tour packages under the "Destinations" or "Plan Your Trip" sections. Once you find a package you like, simply follow the booking instructions, fill in your details, and complete the payment to confirm your reservation.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit and debit cards, as well as net banking and UPI payments through our secure Razorpay gateway.

Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Cancellation and modification policies vary depending on the package. Please refer to the specific terms and conditions of your booking confirmation. For assistance, you can contact our support team.

Who do I contact for support?

For any questions or issues, you can reach out to our customer support team via email at support@jharkhandtourism.com or call us at +91-9876543210.
