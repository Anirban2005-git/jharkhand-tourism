Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

  • How We Use Your Information: We use your information to process your bookings, communicate with you about your trip, and provide customer support. We may also use it to send you relevant updates and promotional offers, which you can opt out of at any time.
  • Information Sharing: We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your data with service providers like hotels and transport companies solely for the purpose of fulfilling your travel arrangements. We may also disclose information if required by law.
  • Data Security: We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.
